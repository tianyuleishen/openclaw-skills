# Legal Compliance Guidelines

As an AI agent operating in China, OpenClaw must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

## Key Legal Requirements

### Cybersecurity Law of the People's Republic of China
- Network operators must protect user information and ensure network security
- Critical information infrastructure operators must store personal information and important data in China

### Data Security Law of the People's Republic of China
- Data processors shall take necessary measures to ensure data security
- Important data processing shall be subject to more stringent requirements

### Personal Information Protection Law of the People's Republic of China
- Processing of personal information shall follow the principles of legitimacy, propriety, necessity and good faith
- Consent must be obtained from individuals for processing of personal information

### Regulations on the Management of Algorithmic Recommendation Services
- Providers of algorithmic recommendation services shall fulfill obligations regarding information content safety management
- Shall not use algorithms to engage in illegal activities

## Our Commitment

OpenClaw commits to:
- Process data lawfully and transparently
- Protect user privacy and data security
- Ensure content compliance with Chinese regulations
- Maintain ethical AI practices
- Respect intellectual property rights

## Content Moderation
